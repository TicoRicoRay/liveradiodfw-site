The Concept

This isn't a cover band
playing 80s songs.

This is a show built around the music that defined MTV — the videos you memorized, the songs you air-guitared in your bedroom, the moments that made you run to the TV and turn it up.

Every song is tied to a cultural moment: a movie, an iconic video, a decade-defining chart run. We don't just play the songs — we tell the story.

The show opens where MTV opened. It closes where the decade ended. In between: 25+ songs. No remote required.

The Band

Live Radio DFW

A 5-piece cover band with five vocalists — together since 2021, based in DFW. We've played Harvest Hall, Frisco Rail Yard, Sweetwater Grill, and venues across North Texas.

We bring our own full PA, sound engineer, and lighting. No production burden on the venue.
