About This Show

Harry Myers Park is a city park in Rockwall at 815 E Washington Street, with a pavilion, open green space, and grounds used for community festivals and outdoor events. Rockwall sits on the shores of Lake Ray Hubbard east of Dallas. An October Saturday evening outdoor fall show for the lakeshore community.
