About This Show

Live Radio DFW plays the Glen Lawn at The Village Dallas - the outdoor community green at one of Dallas's landmark apartment communities in the Skillman/Abrams corridor of east Dallas. This August Saturday show puts the band right in the heart of a neighborhood that loves live music. Family-friendly, a great evening in east Dallas. Come join us.
