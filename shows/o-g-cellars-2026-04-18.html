About This Show

Live Radio DFW brings the party to North Texas wine country. OG Cellars is a boutique winery set in the rolling hills of Sunset, TX — about an hour northwest of the Metroplex. Grab a glass of Texas wine, spread out a blanket on the lawn, and settle in for a night of all 80s hits under the open sky. Food vendors will be on-site for the concert, and the winery's tasting room will be pouring all evening. Gates open early, so come for the sunset and stay for the music.
