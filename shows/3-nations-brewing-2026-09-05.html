Saturday September 5, 2026 · 7:00 PM · Free

About This Show

Live Radio DFW rolls into 3 Nations Brewing in old downtown Carrollton for a free Saturday night of 70s and 80s classic rock, with a few surprises mixed in. This is exactly the kind of room we love: a craft brewery with an outdoor stage, a full kitchen on site, plenty of indoor and covered seating if the Texas weather has opinions, and enough room for the whole family (yes, dogs too). 3 Nations opened in 2015 and moved into this big downtown Carrollton spot in 2019, tucked in the shadow of an old grain mill and just off I-35. Show starts at 7:00 PM. No cover. A great night out for folks in Carrollton, Farmers Branch, Addison, Lewisville, Coppell, and anyone heading up or down the 35 corridor.
