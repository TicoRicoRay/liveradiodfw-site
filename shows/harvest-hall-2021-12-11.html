About This Show

Harvest Hall is a 20,000-square-foot food and entertainment destination at the corner of Main Street and Dallas Road in downtown Grapevine, with eight chef-driven kitchens and the Third Rail listening room inside. Shows like this December Saturday set are part of the holiday-season programming at a venue steps from the Grapevine TEXRail station.
