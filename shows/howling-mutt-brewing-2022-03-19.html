About This Show

Howling Mutt Brewing was a neighborhood brewpub on N Cedar Street in Denton, steps from the Downtown Denton Square. A March Saturday show at this now-closed taproom puts the band in one of DFW's most music-friendly cities, with house-brewed beers on tap in a walkable downtown setting.
