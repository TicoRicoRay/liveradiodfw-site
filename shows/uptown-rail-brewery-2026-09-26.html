About This Show

Live Radio DFW heads to Uptown Rail Brewery in historic Downtown Sanger. This craft brewery has 30+ beers on tap, artisanal pizza, and a stage that puts you right in the action. About 45 minutes north of the Metroplex on I-35, it's worth the drive for the beer alone - add live music and you've got a full Saturday night. No cover charge. Weather permitting, the show moves to the outdoor stage. A fun night out for folks in Sanger, Denton, Lake Dallas, Corinth, and the I-35 corridor.
