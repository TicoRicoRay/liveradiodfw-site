About This Show

Live Radio DFW heads to American Legion Post 597 on S Elm Street in downtown Carrollton for a February Saturday show. This community institution has been here since 1947 - a veterans hall with deep roots in old Carrollton that opens its doors for live music nights and community events. A genuine neighborhood evening for the Carrollton and Farmers Branch communities. Come join us.
