About This Show

Live Radio DFW heads back to O G Cellars in Sunset, TX for a Saturday night 80s Dance Party. This boutique winery sits in the rolling hills of Northwest Wise County and pours 100% Texas wines, with an on-site pizzeria that'll be open through the show. Outdoor and indoor seating, OG wine, frozen drinks, and beer on site (no outside alcohol). Tickets are $25, and it's about an hour northwest of Fort Worth on Hwy 287 - a scenic drive out through the Hill-Country-feeling part of North Texas for fans in the DFW Metroplex, Decatur, Bowie, and Wichita Falls.
