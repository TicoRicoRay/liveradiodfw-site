About This Show

Live Radio DFW takes over the Frisco Rail Yard in Downtown Frisco's Rail District. This open-air food truck park is the perfect spot for a Saturday night out - rotating food trucks, a full bar, and plenty of room to spread out. Bring the crew (dogs welcome too) and grab a table or post up near the stage. No cover charge. The Rail District is walkable and worth exploring before and after the show.
