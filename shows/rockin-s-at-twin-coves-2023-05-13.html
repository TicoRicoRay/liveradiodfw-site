About This Show

Rockin' S at Twin Coves Marina sits on the wooded shore of Lake Grapevine in Flower Mound, offering lakeside dining and live music on weekend evenings. A May Saturday late-afternoon show at this venue is a spring debut at the marina, with the lake visible from the outdoor seating area.
