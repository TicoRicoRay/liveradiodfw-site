About This Show

Meet us at Sweetwater Grill on Hwy 276 between Royse City and Union Valley for a Friday night show on the back patio. One of our go-to spots east of Dallas - full bar, American comfort food, yard games, and a welcoming crowd that knows how to have a good time. About 30 minutes east of Dallas. No cover charge. A great winter Friday night out for the Royse City, Rockwall, Heath, and Fate communities.
