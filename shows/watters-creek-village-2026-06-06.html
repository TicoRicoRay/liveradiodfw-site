About This Show

Live Radio DFW joins the Concerts by the Creek series at Watters Creek Village in Allen. Bring a blanket or lawn chair and stake out a spot on the Village Green - the creek-side setting is one of the best outdoor music venues in Collin County. Restaurants surrounding the green offer patio dining if you'd rather grab a table. Free admission, family-friendly, and a great way to kick off a summer Saturday evening in North Allen.
