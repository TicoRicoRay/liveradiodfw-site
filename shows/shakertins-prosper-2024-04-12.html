About This Show

Live Radio DFW heads to Shakertins on South Preston Road in Prosper for a Friday evening show. This locally owned bar and restaurant in fast-growing Prosper hosts regular live music nights and draws a crowd from all along the Preston Road corridor - Prosper, Celina, and north Frisco. Open late, full bar, good food. No cover charge. Come join us for a night in one of Collin County's newest hot spots.
