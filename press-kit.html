Live Radio DFW

Covering Hits from Classics to Today

Live Radio DFW is the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex's premier variety cover band — a high-energy 5-piece act covering 100+ songs from 6 decades. From 80s synth-pop anthems to Motown soul, classic rock deep cuts to arena-shaking power ballads, we deliver themed "Station" shows tailored to any event. With five vocalists, coordinated video content, in-ear monitors for pristine volume control, and full liability insurance, Live Radio DFW is the professional choice for corporate events, private parties, and venue bookings across North Texas.
